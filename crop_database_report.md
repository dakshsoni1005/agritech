# Gujarat Crops & Image Assets Report

Here is the complete inventory of the 33 crops added to the **Major Crops of Gujarat** directory, categorized into their respective groups, along with the detailed checklist of image assets you should prepare.

---

## 1. Categorized Crops Inventory

### Category 1: Cash Crops & Oilseeds
*   **Cotton (કપાસ)**
    *   **Description**: Primary commercial crop of Gujarat, majorly grown in rich black soils.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Kanam Pradesh and Saurashtra
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Cash Crop, Oilseed
*   **Groundnut / Peanut (મગફળી)**
    *   **Description**: Major Kharif and Summer oilseed. Extensively cultivated in Saurashtra region.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Saurashtra (Rajkot, Junagadh, Amreli)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Summer, Oilseed
*   **Castor Seed (દિવેલા)**
    *   **Description**: Gujarat is India's largest producer and a global leader in castor oil production.
    *   **Growing Regions**: North Gujarat and Saurashtra
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Cash Crop, Oilseed
*   **Sesame / Til (તલ)**
    *   **Description**: Vibrant oilseed grown during multiple seasons including Kharif, Semi-Rabi, and Summer.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Saurashtra and Kutch
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Rabi, Summer, Oilseed
*   **Mustard (રાયડો)**
    *   **Description**: Major winter oilseed crop yielding high quality edible oil.
    *   **Growing Regions**: North Gujarat (Banaskantha, Patan)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Rabi, Oilseed
*   **Sugarcane (શેરડી)**
    *   **Description**: Cultivated in well-irrigated rich black soils with high sugar recovery.
    *   **Growing Regions**: South Gujarat (Surat, Bardoli, Navsari)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Cash Crop, Kharif
*   **Tobacco (તમાકુ)**
    *   **Description**: Commercial crop mainly grown in Charotar region with specific curing processes.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Anand and Kheda districts (Charotar region)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Cash Crop, Rabi

---

### Category 2: Food Grains (Cereals & Millets)
*   **Wheat (ઘઉં)**
    *   **Description**: Dominant Rabi cereal crop. Cultivated extensively under irrigated conditions.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Ahmedabad (Bhal tract), Saurashtra, North Gujarat
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Rabi, Cereal
*   **Paddy / Rice (ડાંગર)**
    *   **Description**: Requires high rainfall and canal irrigated soils for transplantation.
    *   **Growing Regions**: South and Central Gujarat (Kheda, Anand, Surat)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Cereal
*   **Bajra / Pearl Millet (બાજરી)**
    *   **Description**: Drought-resistant nutritious millet crop suitable for sandy and semi-arid soils.
    *   **Growing Regions**: North Gujarat, Banaskantha, Kutch
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Summer, Cereal
*   **Maize / Corn (મકાઈ)**
    *   **Description**: Staple grain grown extensively across the hilly and tribal belts.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Eastern Tribal Belt (Dahod, Panchmahal, Sabarkantha)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Cereal
*   **Jowar / Sorghum (જુવાર)**
    *   **Description**: Grown for human food grains as well as nutritious green dairy fodder.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Saurashtra and North Gujarat
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Cereal

---

### Category 3: Pulses (Kathol)
*   **Tur / Arhar (તુવેર)**
    *   **Description**: Major Kharif pulse crop. Essential in daily Gujarati diets.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Central and Vadodara regions
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Pulse
*   **Gram / Chana / Chickpea (ચણા)**
    *   **Description**: Major Rabi pulse crop. Highly responsive to residual winter moisture.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Ahmedabad (Bhal), Saurashtra, Panchmahal
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Rabi, Pulse
*   **Moong / Green Gram (મગ)**
    *   **Description**: Short duration crop grown during monsoon and summer seasons.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Kutch, Saurashtra and Central Gujarat
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Summer, Pulse
*   **Urad / Black Gram (અડદ)**
    *   **Description**: Kharif pulse requiring moderate rainfall and well-drained soils.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Central and South Gujarat
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Pulse
*   **Moth Bean (મઠ)**
    *   **Description**: Highly drought-tolerant pulse cultivated in extremely arid sandy regions.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Kutch and Banaskantha
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Pulse
*   **Cowpea (ચોળા)**
    *   **Description**: Nutritious pulse suitable for dry climates with minimal irrigation.
    *   **Growing Regions**: North Gujarat and Saurashtra
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Pulse

---

### Category 4: Spices & Condiments
*   **Cumin / Jeera (જીરું)**
    *   **Description**: Highly valuable winter spice crop requiring dry climates during maturity.
    *   **Growing Regions**: North Gujarat and Saurashtra (Gondal, Unjha)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Rabi, Spice
*   **Fennel / Variyali (વરિયાળી)**
    *   **Description**: Gujarat leads nationally in fennel yield and aromatic oil content.
    *   **Growing Regions**: North Gujarat (Mehsana, Patan)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Rabi, Spice
*   **Coriander / Dhana (ધાણા)**
    *   **Description**: Popular winter spice crop cultivated for seeds and green leaves.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Saurashtra and Central Gujarat
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Rabi, Spice
*   **Garlic (લસણ)**
    *   **Description**: Major bulb spice crop cultivated commercially under irrigation.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Saurashtra (Gondal, Rajkot)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Rabi, Spice, Vegetable
*   **Onion (ડુંગળી)**
    *   **Description**: Cultivated for domestic use and dehydration processing units.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Bhavnagar (Mahuva), Junagadh, Rajkot
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Rabi, Kharif, Spice, Vegetable
*   **Fenugreek / Methi (મેથી)**
    *   **Description**: Nutritious winter spice crop grown for seed spices and leafy greens.
    *   **Growing Regions**: North and Central Gujarat
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Rabi, Spice
*   **Chillies (મરચાં)**
    *   **Description**: Cultivated extensively across multiple climatic zones, yielding high pungency.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Central and South Gujarat
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Kharif, Spice

---

### Category 5: Horticulture (Fruits & Vegetables)
*   **Mango (કેરી - ગીર કેસર)**
    *   **Description**: World-famous Gir Kesar mango from Junagadh and Alphonso varieties from South Gujarat.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Junagadh (Talala), Valsad, Navsari
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Fruit, Summer
*   **Banana (કેળું)**
    *   **Description**: Grown under heavy irrigation and organic fertigation schedules.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Central and South Gujarat (Anand, Bharuch, Surat)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Fruit
*   **Sapota / Chikoo (ચીકુ)**
    *   **Description**: Orchard fruit requiring tropical climates and clayey coastal soils.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Valsad and Navsari (Gandevi)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Fruit
*   **Papaya (પપૈયા)**
    *   **Description**: Fast-growing fruit crop yielding throughout the year under drip systems.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Central Gujarat
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Fruit
*   **Pomegranate (દાડમ)**
    *   **Description**: Cultivated in arid regions using modern micro-irrigation systems.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Kutch and Banaskantha
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Fruit
*   **Citrus Fruits (લીંબુ)**
    *   **Description**: Commercial cultivation of high acid lemons and acid limes.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Mehsana and Anand
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Fruit
*   **Potato (બટાટા)**
    *   **Description**: Banaskantha (Deesa) is India's leading processing potato cultivation district.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Banaskantha (Deesa), Sabarkantha
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Vegetable, Rabi
*   **Tomato (ટમેટા)**
    *   **Description**: Popular vegetable crop cultivated across both monsoon and winter cycles.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Gandhinagar, Kheda, Anand
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Vegetable, Rabi, Kharif
*   **Brinjal (રીંગણ)**
    *   **Description**: Cultivated in almost all soil conditions throughout the year.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Central Gujarat
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Vegetable
*   **Okra / Bhindi (ભીંડા)**
    *   **Description**: Cultivated in summer and monsoon seasons, highly demanded in retail markets.
    *   **Growing Regions**: South Gujarat and Kheda
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Vegetable, Summer, Kharif

### Category 6: Medicinal & Industrial Crops
*   **Isabgul / Psyllium Husk (ઈસબગુલ)**
    *   **Description**: Gujarat is the leading global exporter of high-grade Psyllium husk.
    *   **Growing Regions**: North Gujarat (Mehsana, Patan)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Medicinal, Rabi
*   **Guar Seed (ગુવાર બીજ)**
    *   **Description**: Guar gum extracted is used extensively in textile, pharmaceutical, and oil drilling.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Kutch and Banaskantha
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Cash Crop, Industrial, Kharif

---

### Category 7: Floriculture
*   **Marigold (ગલગોટા)**
    *   **Description**: Widely cultivated flower crop used for festive, religious and decorative purposes.
    *   **Growing Regions**: Central and South Gujarat (Anand, Navsari)
    *   **Sowing Tags**: Floriculture, Kharif, Rabi
